Shipping Policy

Thank you for visiting and shopping at Vi Microsystems. Following are the terms and conditions that constitute our Shipping Policy.

Domestic Shipping Policy (India)

  • Free shipping on all orders within India.
  • We ship through DTDC/Professional Courier.
  • All orders will be shipped within 1–3 working days.
  • Estimated delivery time: 2–3 working days from the date of shipping.
  • Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.
  • Delivery delays can occasionally occur for some remote locations.

Shipment Confirmation & Order Tracking

You will receive a Shipment Confirmation email once your order has shipped, containing your tracking number(s). The tracking number will be active within 24 hours.

International Shipping Policy

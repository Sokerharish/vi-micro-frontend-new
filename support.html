About Vi Microsystems

Vi Microsystems Pvt. Ltd. is a pioneer in engineering educational lab equipment in India. Since our founding, we have been committed to delivering high-quality, industry-relevant training platforms to engineering students, research scholars, and industry professionals.

Our product range includes advanced robotics platforms (ROS2-based), networking and cyber security trainers, DSP-based power electronics controllers, and edge AI systems. All products are indigenously designed and manufactured in India.

Vi Microsystems products are used in over 200+ engineering institutions, IITs, NITs, deemed universities, and industry R&D labs across India and abroad.
